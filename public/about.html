About Us

Mockbuster is a retro-inspired movie browsing and rental-style web application, created as part of our CSC 337 course project. The goal of Mockbuster is to recreate the nostalgic experience of exploring classic video rental stores — but reimagined for the modern web.

Users can browse curated collections of Action and Horror movies, save their favorites, manage lists, and interact with a vibrant, neon-styled interface that blends old-school charm with sleek digital design.

Every design choice — from the color scheme to the animated UI — helps bring back the retro energy of movie rental culture while delivering a smooth, intuitive user experience.
