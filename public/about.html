About Us

Mockbuster is a movie browsing and rental-style web application created as part of our CSC 337 course project. Our goal is to allow users to explore movies by genre in a fun and simple way inspired by classic video rental stores.

Users can browse action and horror movies, manage their lists, and experience a clean, retro-style movie interface.
